Classifying the categories of Mass (BMI)

The World Health Organization (WHO) classifies adults based on the following BMI ranges:

  • Underweight: < 18.5 kg/m^2

  • Normal weight: 18.5 - 24.9 kg/m^2

  • Overweight: 25.0 - 29.9 kg/m^2

  • Obese: 30.0 kg/m^2

Cch 1: One-Sample T-test

###gi thuyt BMI (Ch s khi c th) trung bnh 31 d mc bnh tiu ng

# V biu  histogram
ggplot(diabetes, aes(x = BMI)) +
  geom_histogram(binwidth = 2, fill = "lightblue", color = "black") + 
  labs(x = "BMI", title = "Histogram of BMI") + theme_minimal() 

# summary statistics
kable(summary(select(diabetes, BMI)), format = "markdown")

###gi thuyt BMI trung bnh mc bnh tiu ng l 31

\[ H_0: bmi = 31 \] \[ H_a: bmi \neq 31 \]

t.test(diabetes$BMI, mu=31)

Kt lun v gii thch

  • T u ra v t p.value chng ta thy rng
  • p-value nh mc ngha 5%.
  • Ging nh bt k bi kim tra thng k no khc , nu p-value nh hn mc ngha th c th bc b gi thuyt. > Da vo kt qu kim nh t-test, c th kt lun rng gi tr trung bnh thc s ca ct BMI khng bng 31, v c lng trung bnh ca mu c th nm trong khong t 31.43410 n 32.55106 vi mc tin cy 95%.

t.test(normal,sick,var.equal=FALSE)
